/ 05 — About the studio

SloWeb Studio is a small team of young and ambitious web developers from Maribor, Slovenia.

We build websites for businesses, events, brands, and personal projects. Our focus is clean design, fast performance, mobile-friendly layouts, and websites that are easy to maintain.

Because we are a small team, we work closely with every client. We listen to your idea, plan the structure, build the website, and help you keep it updated after launch.
